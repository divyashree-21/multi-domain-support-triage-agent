Q: Payment failed but money deducted
A: Contact your bank to initiate a refund or verify transaction status.

Q: Card declined
A: Ensure sufficient balance and check with your bank for restrictions.

Q: Unauthorized transaction
A: Report immediately to your bank to block the card and initiate investigation.

Q: Dispute a charge
A: You can raise a chargeback request through your bank.

Q: Refund not received
A: Refund timelines depend on the merchant and bank processing.

Q: Lost or stolen card
A: Immediately block your card through your bank.

Q: International transaction issues
A: Ensure your card is enabled for international usage.

Q: Minimum spend requirement
A: Some merchants may have minimum transaction limits.

Q: Cash withdrawal
A: Use ATM with your PIN to withdraw cash.

Q: Fraud alert
A: Contact your bank immediately for suspicious activity.
